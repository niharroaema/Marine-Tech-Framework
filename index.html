1. Statutory & Fiscal Architecture

The foundation of this ecosystem is the Section 8 Company—a non-profit vehicle designed for perpetual succession and tax-efficient reinvestment. Unlike a Trust, a Section 8 entity offers high corporate governance standards, essential for attracting Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funding from major shipyards.

3. Tri-State Vocational Frameworks

Implementation depends on navigating the specific skilling architectures of Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, and West Bengal. Each state offers unique public-private partnership models and digital admission platforms.

World Skill Center (WSC)

Finishing school for ITI/Diploma graduates using Singapore-ITEES pedagogy. Hub for advanced mechatronics.

SAMS Portal

Unified merit-based online selection for all vocational seats. Essential for trainee mobilization.

Sudakshya Scheme

Inclusion program providing 30% horizontal reservation and fee waivers for women in engineering.

Siemens t-SDI

Specialized labs for CNC, Welding, and Automation. Ideal for MRO-specific training modules.

3DEXPERIENCE CoE

Dassault Systemes partnership for cloud-based marine piping design and structural FEA labs.

ICSTP Program

30-45 day industry-customized training inside active yards with direct absorption models.

Utkarsh Bangla

Flagship digitized partner onboarding. Rigorous Training-of-Trainer (TOT) certification mandate.

Advanced Training Centers

Policy allowing CSR-funded sector-specific ATCs inside Govt ITIs or industrial parks.

IIP Module

Lead industries set up specialized in-house labs within active ITIs for direct recruitment.

4. National Flagship Scaling Models

Scaling the Section 8 SPV requires leveraging DGT Flexi-MoU and PM-SETU. These schemes provide the certification authority and capital grants needed for hub-and-spoke infrastructure development.

DGT Flexi-MoU (Employer-Skiller)

  • ITP designs specialized curricula (6-24 months).
  • Mixes theoretical study with intensive shop-floor OJT.
  • Mandate: Guarantee 50% placement for successful graduates.
  • ITP manages practical assessment; DGT handles computerized theory exams.

PM-SETU (Funding Mix)

Financial Model: Central (50%) | State (33%) | Industry (17%)

5. Phased 24-Month Roadmap

A strategic rollout plan to move from legal incorporation to a certified maritime talent pool. Use the timeline below to explore specific milestones for each phase.

1

Phase I: Foundation (Months 1–4)

Incorporate Section 8 SPV, obtain PAN/TAN, and secure provisional 12A/80G status. Focus on drafting MoA/AoA with clear maritime R&D objectives.

2

Phase II: Alliances (Months 5–8)

Execute Tech Transfer with NIOT/NRDC for AUV telemetry. Submit DGT Flexi-MoU application and coordinate with state agencies for portal integration.

3

Phase III: Lab Upgrades (Months 9–12)

Commission mechatronics workstations, AUV testing rigs, and AWS-certified welding bays. Deploy 3DEXPERIENCE design suites for naval architecture.

4

Phase IV: Execution (Months 13–18)

Screen candidates via SAMS/Utkarsh Bangla. Deliver 3-month advanced labs followed by 6-month OJT at partner yards like GRSE or Vizag Shipyards.

5

Phase V: Certification (Months 19–24)

Conduct joint NCVT assessments. Transition provisional tax status to 5-year regular status via Form 10AB. Secure long-term recurring CSR cycles.
